What are the office hours of the registrar's office?	Office hours are Monday to Friday, from 8 AM to 5 PM.
What should I do in case of lost documents?	Go to the registrar's office with a police report to issue new documents.
Where can I request my academic transcript?	The academic transcript can be requested through the student portal, under the "Documents" tab.
Does the university provide a student ID card?	Yes, you can request the ID card through the portal and pick it up at the registrar's office.
How do I schedule an in-person appointment?	Appointments can be made on the university website in the service section.
What documents do I need to request a transfer?	You must present your academic transcript, enrollment proof, and identification document.
How do I request a certificate of completion?	The certificate can be requested through the student portal or directly at the registrar's office.
Is remote service available?	Yes, the university offers remote service via chat and video call.
How can I check internship opportunities offered by the university?	Internship vacancies are available on the student portal in the internships section.
